# 🎓 Learning

A shared space for the PM team to discover, share, and track learning courses related to AI, product management, and working effectively with tools like Claude Code.

---

## 📌 Purpose

There's a lot of great content out there — courses, certifications, tutorials, and reading lists. This folder is where we pool those discoveries so the whole team benefits, not just the person who found it first.

---

## 🗂️ What Belongs Here

- Online courses (Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, Udemy, etc.)
- Certifications relevant to AI or product management
- Tutorials and guided walkthroughs
- Curated learning paths (e.g. "AI for PMs in 4 weeks")
- Short summaries or notes from courses you've completed
- Recommended reading lists

## ✍️ How to Contribute

Edit this README and add a row to the table above. Use the template below if you'd like to add a longer write-up as a separate file in this folder.

### Course Entry Template

```markdown
## [Course Name]
**Added by:** [Your name]
**Link:** [URL]
**Format:** [Video / Text / Interactive]
**Time commitment:** [e.g. 4 hours, self-paced]

### What It Covers
A short summary of the course content.

### Why It's Worth Your Time
What did you get out of it? Who would benefit most?

### Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]
- [Takeaway 3]
```

---

## 💡 Tips

> **Start with a summary.** Even a 3-bullet takeaway from a course you completed helps teammates decide whether it's worth their time.

> **Link to free content where possible.** Not everyone has the same subscriptions — flag if something requires a paid account.

> **Quality over quantity.** Only share things you've found genuinely useful. This isn't a bookmarks dump.
