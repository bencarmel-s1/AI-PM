## SLIDE 7: H1 2026 Roadmap — Q1
**Embed AI Across the Investigation Workflow**

*[Visual: Timeline with 3 parallel tracks — January / February / March]*

**Initiative 1: AI Alert Scoring & Context** *(January)*
- Every alert gets an AI-generated severity score + plain-language explanation
- Reduces tier-1 triage time from 8 min to <5 min

**Initiative 2: Natural Language Threat Hunting** *(February)*
- Type a question in plain English — get results without writing a detection query
- Lowers the skill floor for mid-market teams without dedicated threat hunters

**Initiative 3: AI Morning Briefing** *(March)*
- Daily push: open critical alerts, stalled investigations, new relevant threat patterns
- Push intelligence — no query required

**Q1 success metric:** AI engagement >45% of daily active analysts

---

## SLIDE 8: H1 2026 Roadmap — Q2
**Deepen, Iterate, and Build the Moat**

*[Visual: Timeline with 3 parallel tracks — April / May / June]*

**Initiative 4: Personalized Threat Profiles** *(April)*
- After 60 days telemetry: AI learns what's normal for each customer's environment
- "This process has never run on any endpoint in your environment in 90 days."
- **This is the moat** — requires customer-specific data new entrants don't have

**Initiative 5: Automated Incident Summaries** *(May)*
- AI drafts incident report from investigation timeline — analyst reviews and sends
- Saves 45–60 min per incident; addresses #1 pain point: reporting overhead

**Initiative 6: AI Containment Recommendations** *(June)*
- During active investigation: AI suggests containment steps with one-click approval
- Builds on existing autonomous response infrastructure

**Q2 success metric:** 30-day retention of AI-active accounts >85%
