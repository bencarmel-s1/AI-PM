# Strategic Planning Skills

Use these to sharpen decisions before you commit — whether you're aligning on roadmap priorities or making a case for a direction.

---

## Skills in This Folder

| Skill | What it helps you do |
|---|---|
| `strategic-decision-validation.md` | Pressure-test a product decision against your strategy before presenting or committing |
| `backlog-prioritization.md` | Bring structure to messy backlogs and articulate clear prioritization rationale |

---

## When to use these

Pull these guides before leadership reviews, quarterly planning, or any moment where you need to get confident and aligned before moving fast.

---

## Example

```
@strategic-planning/strategic-decision-validation.md
We're about to commit to rebuilding the onboarding flow. Here's the rationale: @decision-brief.md
What assumptions are we making and where are we most exposed?
```
