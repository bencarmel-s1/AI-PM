## Q1 FY26 Final (Ended Mar 31, 2026)

### O1: Accelerate Customer Activation
| KR | Target | Final | Result |
|----|--------|-------|--------|
| KR1.1 Time-to-first-value | ≤ 7 days | 11 days | 🔴 Missed |
| KR1.2 Onboarding completion rate | ≥ 80% | 64% | 🔴 Missed |
| KR1.3 Activation NPS | ≥ 45 | 38 | 🔴 Missed |

**Q1 Retrospective Note:** The onboarding KRs were all missed due to a combination of empty-state UX issues and a 14-step checklist that users were abandoning. Customer interviews in Q1 confirmed this. Q2 onboarding revamp project directly addresses this.

### O2: Drive Feature Adoption & Stickiness
| KR | Target | Final | Result |
|----|--------|-------|--------|
| KR2.1 Alerts feature adoption | ≥ 60% MAU | 47% | 🔴 Missed |
| KR2.2 Integrations per account | ≥ 3 | 1.8 | 🔴 Missed |
| KR2.3 D30 retention | ≥ 72% | 69% | 🟡 Near miss |

**Q1 Retrospective Note:** Alerts adoption is lagging because new users don't discover the feature — it's buried in the nav. In-app discovery (tooltip + empty-state) is the Q2 lever. Integrations per account is a longer-term problem requiring catalog v2 (Q3 target).

### O3: Establish Analytics as a Strategic Platform

| KR3.2 Platform NPS | ≥ 50 | 44 | 🟡 Near miss |
