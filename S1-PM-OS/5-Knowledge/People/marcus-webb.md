## Processing Style
Marcus thinks in systems and dependencies. Before committing to anything, he maps what could go wrong downstream. He will push back hard on timeline pressure if he thinks it compromises quality or creates technical debt.

When he says "I need to think about it," that means he wants 24 hours — don't push for an answer in the room.

## How to Work Well with Marcus
- Send him the problem statement + relevant context 24h before any sync where you need a decision
- When presenting new features, lead with the user problem and expected impact, then ask for his read on complexity
- If you need a faster timeline than his estimate, explain *why* (customer commitment, OKR risk) — don't just push back on the number
- Acknowledge technical debt concerns explicitly, even if you're not solving them now
