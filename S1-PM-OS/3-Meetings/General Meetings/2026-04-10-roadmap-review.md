## Key Discussions

**Onboarding timeline risk**
Marcus raised the smart defaults backend concern again. Olivia pushed back on any slip — she has 3 pipeline deals where the onboarding story is a key objection.

Decision: We will ship a "v1 without smart defaults" on May 30 and ship smart defaults as a follow-on in June. This protects the launch date and gives Sales something concrete to point to.

**Alerts adoption campaign**
Tyler asked for early access to the in-app tooltip for CS coaching purposes. Agreed — Tyler's team gets preview access 1 week before GA (Apr 23 internal, Apr 30 GA).

**Integration Catalog v2 scope**
Olivia asked if Shopify integration (3 customer requests now) could be prioritized. Current constraint: eng capacity through Q2. We aligned that Shopify moves to Q3 if we can get design partner validation in May.

**Design partner pipeline**
Currently 1 committed design partner (Globex Corp). Need 2 more for OKR KR3.1. Sarah offered intros to Nexus Health and Pinnacle Financial. Target: 3 committed by June 30.
