# Customer Discovery — RetailMax | Mar 18, 2026

## Objective
Understand RetailMax's activation experience and surface blockers to expanding Pulse adoption from their marketing team to their product team.

---

## Customer Context
RetailMax is an e-commerce platform with ~800 employees. They've been on Pulse for 8 months. Currently 2 active seats (marketing analysts). Renewal in September. Tier: Mid-Market.

---

## Key Discussion Points

**Onboarding experience**
Dana shared that when they first got access, the empty dashboard was confusing. "We didn't know what we were supposed to do first." They stumbled through setup and it took about 3 weeks before they felt confident using the product.

Key quote: *"The product is powerful once you're in it, but the front door is intimidating."*

- They skipped several onboarding checklist steps because the labels weren't clear
- They didn't discover the Alerts feature until month 4 — only found it because their CSM mentioned it

**Integration blockers**
Their #1 expansion blocker: Pulse doesn't have a native Shopify integration. They're using a custom webhook workaround, which Raj said breaks every time Shopify updates their API.

- They asked if a Shopify connector is on the roadmap
- Two other customers in their peer group have the same pain (according to Tyler)

**What they love**
- Session replay — "this changed how our team does QA"
- Funnel analysis — easy to build, non-technical team members can do it
- The Slack digest (daily summary notification)
