# Productivity Skills

For the work of managing your own focus — when the to-do list is long but not all of it matters.

---

## Skills in This Folder

| Skill | What it helps you do |
|---|---|
| `todo-prioritization.md` | Cut through a busy to-do list to identify what deserves your attention today vs. later |
| `multi-agent-patterns.md` | Run all 3 agents in parallel on a spec — when to use subagents vs agent teams |

---

## When to use these

At the start of your week or day, especially in high-stakes periods where the wrong use of your time has real cost.

---

## Example

```
@productivity/todo-prioritization.md
Here's what I have on my plate this week: @todo_index.md
Help me figure out where to focus.
```
