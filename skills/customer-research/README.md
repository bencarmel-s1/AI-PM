# Customer Research Skills

Tools for making sense of what customers are actually telling you — and sharpening how you ask.

---

## Skills in This Folder

| Skill | What it helps you do |
|---|---|
| `customer-feedback-synthesis.md` | Turn raw feedback and meeting notes into structured themes and decision-ready signals |
| `customer-call-questionnaire.md` | Audit and improve your interview questions to get stronger, more actionable insight |

---

## When to use these

Pull these guides before a customer research sprint, when you're preparing inputs for roadmap reviews, or whenever you're drowning in notes and need to surface what matters.

---

## Example

```
@customer-research/customer-feedback-synthesis.md
Here are 6 customer call notes from this week: @calls/
Synthesize the key themes before our planning session on Thursday.
```
